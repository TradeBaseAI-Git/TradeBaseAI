Overview

TradeBaseAI ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Mychail Solar Energy LLC, doing business as TradeBaseAI. We provide AI automation systems for trade businesses including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and roofing contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website at tradebaseai.com and our services.

By using our website or submitting any form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact or demo request form on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Business name
  • Trade type (e.g. HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Optional message describing how TradeBaseAI can help your business

We may also collect standard technical data automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or schedule a demo
  • To send SMS communications related to your inquiry, appointment reminders, and follow-ups
  • To provide information about our AI automation services
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

SMS Communications

By submitting our contact form and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive SMS messages from TradeBaseAI regarding your inquiry. This may include appointment reminders, follow-ups, and service information.

Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, including:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery infrastructure
  • Airtable — CRM and lead management
  • Google Calendar — appointment scheduling
  • Anthropic — AI response processing
  • n8n — workflow automation

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us and we will process your request within 30 days.

Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by contacting us directly

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
